Honey Sriracha Tofu

Description

This is a recipe for a quick and easy sweet and spicy tofu dish using sriracha and honey.

Make sure you are using extra firm tofu and to dry them out as much as possible before attempting to fry them up.

You can also substitute the tofu with chicken thighs (And I highly recommend it!)

Ingredients

  • 14 oz. extra firm tofu
  • 2 Tbsp honey
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 2 Tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 green onions, sliced
